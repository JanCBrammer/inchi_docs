150 parity (Mask 0x07=BITS_PARITY):

156 6 = AB_PARITY_CALC = calculate later from the neighbor ranks;

159
160 length (Mask 0x38=MASK_CUMULENE_LEN, length=stereo_bond_parity[i]/MULT_STEREOBOND):

164 2 => cumulene with 3 double bonds (stereogenic bond)

168 */
